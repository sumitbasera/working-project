INDIA

India officially the Republic of India is a country in South Asia. It is the seventh-largest country by area, the second-most populous country with over 1.2 billion people, and the most populous democracy in the world. The Indian economy is the worldâ€™s tenth-largest by nominal GDP and third-largest by purchasing power parity (PPP). Following market-based economic reforms in 1991, India became one of the fastest-growing major economies; it is considered a newly industrialized country. India is a federal constitutional republic governed under a parliamentary system consisting of 28 states and 8 union territories. India is a pluralistic, multilingual, and a multi-ethnic society.

STUDY IN INDIA

As we all know that Indian education is known throughout the world, it is because of the fast integrating world economy and corresponding rise of student mobility that have made studying in India an attractive option. India has created an educational environment where emphasis is given not only to theoretical knowledge but also to practical knowledge. Due to which, Information Technology, Business, Science etc are the areas where Indian institutions have been producing the merit candidates. For the Nepalese students India has always been one of the popular education destinations. It is because of the neighboring border and also due to the booming technology, economic development and educational & intellectual advancement. Besides, there are many similarities between India and Nepal in terms of culture, food, language, living and social settings. This greatly helps the students to get acquainted easily in a new environment away from their home. Today many universities /colleges in India are equipped with the latest technologies which are essential for students to acquire knowledge. Students get international class facilities in such institutions comparatively at a highly affordable cost compared to other countries of the world. To honour and encourage the deserving students to pursue their academic career many institutions in India provide partial or full scholarships for the deserving Students. The degree from prestigious institutions in India carries international credibility and even the students get placement ( job) in prestigious companies with handsome compensation packages after their completion of courses from the prestigious Colleges/ Universities. So, there are wide opportunities for the students who want to earn academic qualification from India but the students should have the right information to make decisions for their career endeavours. Our team will guide you to earn your degree from the universities/ colleges of your choice from India.
