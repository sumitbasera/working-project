CANADA

CANADA

Canada is a popular choice among international students due to the country’s amazing natural landscapes, award winning lifestyle and world class education standards. Canada is a peaceful, politically stable and safe country. Canada’s education reputation is based on quality and recognized for excellence across the entire education sector. Canadian credentials are valued around the world. The chance to learn in world-class institutions helps to build up your career ahead. Canada’s tuition fees are among the lowest in English speaking countries. Their education systems boost a wide range of quality educational institutions for degrees and diplomas in technical and professional disciplines. Over 106000 international students attend Canadian colleges and universities. This means that you can meet people from different countries. After completing graduation from a Canadian university or college you can work in Canada for up to one year.
